Lisbon, Portugal

Experience One of Europe's Oldest Cities

As Europe's second oldest capital city, Lisbon, Portugal has been occupied for well over three thousand years and has witnessed the rise and fall of many civilizations. In Lisbon, you'll see the remnants of these civilizations through diverse art and architecture. In Portugal, you will learn particularly about the Age of Discovery, during which the Portugese Empire and its explorers played a major roll. Lisbon's castles, mosques, and cathedrals will tell you the story of European history in a way few other cities can.

The Santiago De Alfama is an award winning historic boutique hotel. Situated in Alfama, Lisbon's oldest neighborhood, the Santiago De Alfama is within walking distance of some of Lisbon's most historic sites.

Rome, Italy

See The Eternal City

Rome has one of the most impressive histories of any city in the world. As capital of the Roman Empire, and the home of Vatican City, its architecture and cultural heritage have had great influence on the modern West. It is a vibrant city with amazing cuisine, and endless monuments.
